Collins Aerospace - Quality Engineering Co-op

Impact

At Collins Aerospace, I worked as a Quality Engineering Intern within the Power and Controls MRO division, collaborating with engineers to improve hardware reliability and system performance. I created a digital inspection tool, reducing customer escapes by 67% and inspection time by 46%, validated through a time study and adopted across multiple sites. I supported the testing department by assisting in the development and documentation of a troubleshooting system that prevented recurring mechanical failures. I automated quality metrics collection using SAP scripting and Excel macros, and built interactive Tableau dashboards to visualize performance trends. Additionally, I led training sessions for interns across North America and Europe on workflow automation tools used in test and design documentation, and created digital forms for document control and audit readiness.
