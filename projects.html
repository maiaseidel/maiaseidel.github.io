APEX @ ASU

What We Do

APEX is a liquid propulsion club focused on designing, building, and testing a turbopump-fed LOX/Kerosene rocket engine. The long term development path includes turbomachinery optimization, regenerative cooling chamber design, and full ground test infrastructure for hot fire campaigns. Our goal is to push beyond pressure fed student rocketry and expand propulsion research access at ASU.
